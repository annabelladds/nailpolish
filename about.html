About
Ey-Nail Polish

Driven by passion since she was young, Ey-Nail Polish was founded by Annabella a.k.a Eybel at March 2021. She started working since she was 19 years old, practicing by her skilss as a nail artist at her friend's salon.

With appropriate skilss, vast amount of experience and her record of achievements, Eybel started her own business, Ey-Nail Polish. Her specialties can be divided into three : fake nails, courses, and nail care services such as manicure and pedicure.
